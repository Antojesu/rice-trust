AIM OF THE TRUST

To build a better society through charitable activities.

OBJECTIVES OF THE TRUST

(Excerpt from the Trust Deed)

1. To help the poor children, their families, and the society in general through sponsorship and educational development programmes so as to grow and develop in dignity, security, and self-fulfillment.

2. To work and to defend the rights of children and human beings, especially the oppressed people, and to promote democracy through developing effective, cooperative domestic and international networks at the civil society level and capacity-building and training programmes.

3. To the advancement of democracy and human rights through print and media programmes.

4. To assist farmers to adopt soil conservation and water management activities with special emphasis on wasteland management and to encourage them to learn and practice appropriate technology in the field of agriculture, animal husbandry, and allied fields.

5. To arrange drinking water facilities, sanitation, road facilities, and implement other rural development activities.

6. To promote and self-help groups among males and females, widows, and farmers for their economic development.

7. To organize old age homes, destitute homes, mercy homes, creches, hostels, mentally challenged homes, cradle baby schemes, technical schools, computer education, AIDS control schemes and camps, diabetic care camps, eye camps, anti-filarial camps, anti-leprosy schemes, village nutrition programmes for pregnant ladies, and any other programme for the development of weaker sections.

8. To help and support the victims of natural calamities such as floods, fire, earthquakes, tsunami, etc.

9. To work so as to ensure that every child completes quality primary and secondary school education.

10. To develop an integrated approach to services that include: health, women's health, nutrition, water and environmental sanitation, psychosocial care, early learning, and child protection.
